Websites

Most of these are works of passion. The more recent ones are contributions to the ever-growing open government movement in Chicago. They are all built with either jQuery, Wordpress or Ruby.

Vacant and Abandoned Building Finder - Chicago »

Launched Sept 30th, 2011

Find vacant and abandoned buildings in Chicago. Data comes from reports made for open and vacant buildings to 311 since January 1, 2010.
Press: article on govfresh | Webitects blog | Apps for Metro Chicago Contest (7th place)

LookAtCook.com »

Launched Aug 17th, 2011

Budget transparency visualization for Cook County, IL displaying all county departments broken down by fund and control officer from 1993 to 2011. Done as a collaboration with 12th District Commissioner John Fritchey. Built with Fusion Tables and jQuery.
Press: Article on O'Reilly Radar | Article on govfresh | Webitects blog

ChicagoLobbyists.org »

Launched July 16th, 2011

A site intended to improve the transparency of interactions between City of Chicago agencies and lobbyists and their clients. All data comes from the City of Chicago and can be found at data.cityofchicago.org. Built in Ruby/Sinatra.
Press: Mayor Emanuel's release on updated lobbyist data | @ChiLobbyists on Twitter

Thought You Knew »

Launched Dec 13th, 2010

Gallery and news site for the Thought You Knew (TyK) bicycle pinup calendar dedicated to helping women become more confident in the bicycling world. Designed by my wife Aya.

Chicago Recycling Dropoff »

Launched June 12th, 2010

My wife Aya and I created a map of all City of Chicago recycling drop off locations. We also set up a mobile site so you can find the nearest drop off point from your current location.

New Years Day Song Project »

Launched Feb 26th, 2010

Every new year’s day since 2006, a group of friends and I write and record a song. They're all very silly.

Maps

These are all done using Google Fusion Tables. Let me know if you have a cool dataset you think I should make a map of. If you're feeling adventurous, you can make one of your own by starting with this.

Health Clinics in Chicago »

Created Oct 26th, 2011

Find Neighborhood, Mental Health and Women, Infants & Children (WIC) clinics in Chicago.

Chicago Bike Crash Reports »

Last updated July 12th, 2011

Searchable map of nearly 5,000 reported bicycle crashes in the City of Chicago from 2007-2009 as reported to the Illinois Department of Transportation. Recently added some more advanced filters.

Cook County Hospitals »

Last updated June 24th, 2011

All hospitals in Cook County compared to ward and neighborhood.

2011 IL Senate Redistricting Plan »

Last updated May 19th, 2011

View the IL senate's redistricting plan released on May 19th, 2011 to see what district you will fall in and compare it to the existing 2001 boundaries.

Chicago TIF Projects »

Last updated Feb 27th, 2011

I teamed up with a group of journalism students from Columbia College to present a searchable map of Tax Increment Financing (TIF) Projects in Chicago. Also includes a heat map of amounts allocated per ward.
Press: New York Times | Chicago News Cooperative | ChicagoTalks

Code

Some codebases I've been working on to help with the above projects.You can find all of these on github.

Searchable Map Template »

Last updated Oct 26th, 2011

After receiving several requests for a walkthrough for creating searchable maps with Fusion Tables, I decided to create this template to help get people started. Get it on github.

Fusion Tables to HTML Table »

Last updated Oct 30th, 2011

Reads in a Fusion Table and outputs it in to a searchable and sortable HTML table using jQuery Datatables. Created as a companion to the above searchable map template. Get it on github.

Socrata to Fusion Tables »

hosted on github

PHP script to scrape a Socrata dataset and import it in to a Google Fusion Table. Example data uses Chicago's 311 requests for vacant and abandoned buildings and is used to update this map.
